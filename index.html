Campus Life

school Scouts and guides

The school has an NCC Air wing for girls and Army wing for Boys besides regular Bharat Scouts & Guides. The students attend various camps to sharpen their skills.

school classroom

Each classroom is well ventilated with adequate natural sunlight and spacious for free movement.

school lab

The school has state of the art physics, Biology, Chemistry, Maths, Language, Psychology, Computers and Social Science Laboratories. Two well stocked libraries to cater to the reading needs of the resource students and teachers.

What's New

  • New clinics added

    Two clinics on the school campus handle any health emergencies while students are at school. Fully trained nurses are in charge of these clinics. Annual eye check-ups or dental check -up by trained doctors are held.


  • New digital classroom

    We have digitally equipped classrooms so as to make the process of teaching and learning interactive and effective.
